About Me

HELLO STRANGER ~ Welcome to my site.

As you may have read my name is Danijela and I am a web developer. Nice to meet you!

I have over 3 years of experience in working with web technologies, but my main focus is a front-end web development. I'm a self-taught programmer and passionate learner with interest in web development and understanding of the entire process (design, development, and deployment). Now I know that the best way to learn something is by solving tasks and complex problems alone or in the team. I get an along fine in a team, work with predefined code standards and maintain the code quality at a high level, but also taking responsibility for my mistakes, helping others and guiding them if necessary.

  • I have experience with web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, JS and Frameworks such as Jquery, React.js, Bootstrap, etc.
  • I like to design and code things from scratch and enjoy bringing ideas to life in the browser.
  • Understanding of UX and Accessibility and optimizing applications for best performance and user experience.
  • I'm focused on frontend development but I also have some knowledge about how to get information out of a database or change a line of PHP.
  • Familiarity with cross-browser and page-load optimization - the basics about the cross-browser optimizations/support, understanding of SEO principles.
  • Proficiency in graphic design software such as Adobe Photoshop and basics of video editing.
  • Familiarity with handling data using AJAX and Jquery, manipulation of DOM.
  • Experience with version control systems (Git, GitHub, GitLab) and with automation tools such as Gulp, as well as with package managers like NPM.
  • Solid understanding debugging using the browser dev-tools.
